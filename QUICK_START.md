# 🚀 QUICK START - Professional Website Builder

## ⚡ Get Started in 30 Seconds

### **Step 1: Find the Builder Button**
👉 Look at the **bottom-right corner** of your screen
👉 See the yellow button that says **"Builder"**?
👉 Click it!

### **Step 2: The Admin Panel Opens**
A professional control panel slides in from the right side with 4 tabs:
- 🎨 **Logo & Branding**
- 📌 **Header Navigation**
- 📑 **Page Sections**
- ⚙️ **Global Settings**

### **Step 3: Start Customizing**

#### **Quick Win #1: Change Your Logo (1 min)**
1. Click the **🎨 Logo & Branding** tab
2. Change **Logo Text** to your company name
3. Pick a **Text Color** and **Icon Color**
4. Click **Save & Apply**
5. Done! ✅

#### **Quick Win #2: Brand Your Header (1 min)**
1. Click the **📌 Header Navigation** tab
2. Choose **Background Color** (try #08080C for dark)
3. Choose **Text Color** (white looks good)
4. Toggle **Sticky Header** if you want it to stay on top
5. Click **Save & Apply**
6. Done! ✅

#### **Quick Win #3: Add a Section (2 min)**
1. Click the **📑 Page Sections** tab
2. Click one of the section type buttons: **+ Hero**, **+ Services**, etc.
3. Click the **▼ Expand** arrow to edit the section
4. Fill in:
   - Section Title
   - Background Color
   - Text Color
   - Heading
   - Description
5. Click **Save & Apply**
6. Done! ✅

## 💾 Saving Your Work

### **Automatic Save** ✅
- All changes save automatically
- No "Save" button needed
- Safe to refresh page

### **Make a Backup**
1. Click **Export Config** button
2. File downloads as `website-config.json`
3. Keep it safe!

### **Restore from Backup**
1. Click **Import Config** button
2. Select your `.json` file
3. Everything restored! ✅

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

| Problem | Fix |
|---------|-----|
| Can't find Builder button | Look bottom-right corner, might need to scroll down |
| Changes not showing | Click "Save & Apply", then refresh page (F5) |
| Logo looks weird | Try using emoji icon, add background color |
| Text too small on mobile | Reduce "Padding" in Global Settings |
| Page too wide on desktop | Reduce "Max Width" in Global Settings |
| Header too tall | Use Header Height slider to shrink it |
